# Privacy Policy — PinnedNote

**Last updated: April 18, 2026**

## Overview

PinnedNote is a simple note-taking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

**Notes content**
Your notes are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync, your notes are synced via Apple iCloud and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

**Purchase information**
In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not track your location
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not use advertising frameworks

## iCloud

If you enable iCloud Sync, your notes are stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is managed by Apple and subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## Lock Screen Widget

Notes pinned to the lock screen are displayed using Apple's Live Activities framework. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.
